Experience

Front-end Work

For the front-end work, we apply css, html, javascript and google map api for the construction of the webpage and finally show what we need to express on the web. At the beginning, we considered for a while whether to build an APP or a website, after some deep consideration, we decided to use the platform which can contain more inforamation and realize more function: webpage. During the whole process, we encountered a great many challenges, such as the design of the web，the establishment of the databse, the marking function of the map and etc. We went through a lot of trials and met with numbers of trouble. We communicated with each other and got the help from the teacher as well as the teammates. Thanks to the careful instruction and the patient cooperatiion, we could finally give people a nice webpage.

Back-end Work

When it comes to the work of back-end, we focus our attention on applying SVM and Random Forests. These two are valuable tools for us to build our own algorithm which can offer us the help to make the prediction. In machine learning, SVMs are supervised learning models with associated learning algorithms that analyze data used for classification and regression analysis. Random Forests are an ensemble learning method for classification that operate by constructing a multitude of decision trees at training time and outputting the class that is the mode of the classes or mean prediction of the individual trees. As a matter of fact, after we have some comparisons, we decided to use Random Forest which can give us a better accuracy. Eventually, we could finally reach the accuracy around 67% which is indeed quite satisfactory.
